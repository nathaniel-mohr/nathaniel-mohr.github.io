Background

My name is Nathaniel Mohr and I am a 21 year old computer scientist. I am originally from Modesto, California, but I currently reside in Corvallis, Oregon.
I was first introduced to the field of Computer Science through two programming courses my sophomore and senior years of high school. I had always had an interest in STEM, but it was these courses that focused my interest to computers and software development.

During my undergraduate, my computer science electives have had a focus in computer graphics, simulations, and the computation and analysis of numbers.
